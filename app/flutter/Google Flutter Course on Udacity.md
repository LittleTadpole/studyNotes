## Google Flutter Course on Udacity

1. flutter Inspector

   > View -> Tool windows-->flutter Inspector, 
   >
   > 可设置显示控件区域,控件baseLine, debug paint(显示所有的布局边距), slow 动画

2. 去掉右上角的debug模式

   ```
   debugShowCheckedModeBanner: false,
   ```

3. 鼠标指向某个控件,然后alt+enter 提示可选择该控件摆放位置
